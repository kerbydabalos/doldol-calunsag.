JASMINE BEACH

Facilities

MODERN BEACHFRONT VILLA

A two-storey villa with sleek glass windows, a wooden accent roof, and a wide balcony. This spacious beachfront home offers comfortable accommodations for families or groups. Surrounded by nature, it’s perfect for relaxing or enjoying ocean views from the balcony.

WATER SPORTS & BOAT LAUNCH BAY

Equipped with a high-powered speedboat labeled “Men At Sea SPORTS” and inflatable rides, this section supports various sea activities. Boats are safely stored under a tarp canopy near the beachfront for quick access and launching.

BEACHSIDE COOKING AREA

A shaded, open-air shelter with built-in concrete tables and benches. Perfect for preparing meals and enjoying food with family and friends. Set under large trees, this rustic area is cool, breezy, and ideal for casual gatherings or reunions.

COTTAGE

This is only ₱450 to ₱500 and is perfect for families who want an affordable and relaxing getaway.
